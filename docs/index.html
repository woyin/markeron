Click-through mode

Toggle drawing and pointer modes from the toolbar or with Ctrl+Shift+X. Annotations remain visible while clicks, scrolls, and typing go to the app underneath.

Keyboard-first tools

Switch tools with number keys, adjust width with Ctrl+scroll, cycle colors with Q/E, and clear or copy without opening menus.

Whiteboard when needed

Press W for a clean whiteboard, keep content between sessions, and copy the result as an image for docs, chats, and notes.

Common questions

For people comparing ZoomIt, Epic Pen, and lightweight whiteboards.

Is MarkerOn a ZoomIt alternative?

MarkerOn focuses on fast annotation, keyboard control, and click-through drawing. It works well alongside Windows Magnifier if you need zoom during a presentation.

Does it have Epic Pen-style mouse mode?

Yes. v1.0 adds click-through mode, so annotations stay visible while you use the apps below, then switch back to drawing whenever needed.

Does it upload screenshots or require an account?

No. MarkerOn is local-first, open source, and does not require an account. Annotation content stays on your machine.
