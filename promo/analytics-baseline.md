## Campaign Measurement

For each campaign, record:

1. Timestamp and exact public URL.
2. UTM source / medium / campaign used.
3. Baseline stars and downloads immediately before posting.
4. Traffic, stars, downloads, issues, and comments at +24h, +7d, and +30d.
5. Qualitative feedback themes and the product changes they influenced.
